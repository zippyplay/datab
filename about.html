About

Clear thinking, careful analysis, and results you can trust.

Datab provides advanced data analytics and modelling services for organisations that need reliable answers from complex or imperfect data.

The focus is practical and rigorous: define the problem clearly, ensure data quality, apply appropriate analytical methods, and communicate results in a way that supports real decisions.

How I work

  • Problem-first, not tool-first
  • Data quality and assumptions made explicit
  • Methods chosen for robustness and interpretability
  • Clear communication for both technical and non-technical audiences

What clients can expect

  • Honest assessment of what the data can and cannot support
  • Reproducible analysis and transparent logic
  • Outputs that can be used, reviewed, and built upon
  • A collaborative, straightforward working style

If you’re looking for analytics that prioritise clarity over hype, We’d be happy to discuss your project.
