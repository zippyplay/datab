Privacy

How we handle personal data.

Databoratory is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law.

What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you choose to provide to us. This typically includes:

  • Your name (if included in your message)
  • Your email address
  • The content of your enquiry

How we use your data

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Communicate with you about potential or ongoing work

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Legal basis

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interest — specifically, responding to enquiries and communicating with prospective clients.

Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or maintain a professional relationship, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. Basic server logs may be generated by our hosting provider for security and operational purposes.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
